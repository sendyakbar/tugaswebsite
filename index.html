ABOUT US

Sendy's Garage is motorbike modification workshop. Other than workshops, we also sells and develop a custom parts and merchendise. Based on these we are trying to introduce modification along with the lifestyle. Do you want to make a custom motorbike? bring your 3 reference pictures. Because if you just bring a single picture and you want you made such a picture that you carry, you do not need our services. Basically, we're not only offered the service of making custom motorbike, but also improving creative idea in making process. Next, based of the reference picture, we will give you the main design which is going to be our guide for making your motorbike.

We are trying to find expression of the most fundamental philosophies of a lifestyle and custom bike. We are not focus on extreme custom but focus to an idea that original and fun. We are not only trying to make just a custom bike. But also more than that, we made lifestyle with a custom bike. Sendy's Garage start from a garage and hobbies, to show our creativity as it is, not start with big and magnificent business plan. Teamwork, friends and freedom togetherness that not bound. We stand with our own culture. We stand with our own style. Maintain identity motorcycle as it is.
